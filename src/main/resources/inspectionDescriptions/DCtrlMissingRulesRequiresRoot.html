Detects implicit legacy "Rules-Requires-Root: binary-targets".

This inspection detects packages that rely on the implicit default of Rules-Requires-Root: binary-targets. Historically, all Debian packages required root or fakeroot to build. As of Debian buster, it is possible and recommended to explicitly declare whether a package need root or fakeroot by setting the Rules-Requires-Root field to either no or binary-targets. Most modern packages will work out of the box with Rules-Requires-Root: no.
